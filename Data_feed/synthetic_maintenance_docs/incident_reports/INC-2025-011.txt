NEAR-MISS REPORT
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Report #: INC-2025-011
Date of Event: 2025-11-14
Reported by: Robert Alvarez
Equipment Involved: CV-220 (Control Valve, Unit 2)

Description:
CV-220 positioner failed following overnight rain, causing the valve
to stick at partial open position. Cooling water flow to Unit 2 was
reduced for approximately 50 minutes before the issue was identified
and the valve was manually adjusted while repairs were made.

Potential Consequence:
Reduced cooling water flow for an extended period could have led to a
process temperature excursion in Unit 2 if not caught promptly. This
is the second such event this year (first was May 2025).

Root Cause Discussion:
Same root cause as the May 2025 event - moisture ingress into the
NEMA 3R positioner enclosure, which is not rated for the valve's
outdoor, rain-exposed mounting location. Gasket-only repairs have not
resolved the underlying issue.

Corrective Actions:
1. Temporary gasket repair performed to restore service (WO-10247).
2. NEMA 4X enclosure upgrade requested - this is now flagged as a
   repeat near-miss and should be prioritized for the next available
   outage window.

Reported per SOP-INC-001.
