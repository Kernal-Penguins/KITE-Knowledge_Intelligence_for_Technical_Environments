NEAR-MISS REPORT
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Report #: INC-2026-002
Date of Event: 2026-02-10
Reported by: Lisa Nguyen
Equipment Involved: TK-301 (Storage Tank 301)

Description:
During a routine fill operation, the level gauge on TK-301 was noted
by the operator to be reading inconsistently with the delivered
volume, requiring a manual dip check to confirm actual level before
continuing the fill. No overfill occurred; fill was paused until the
discrepancy was resolved manually.

Potential Consequence:
An uncorrected level gauge discrepancy during a fill operation could
lead to an overfill event and product loss/environmental release if
not caught by manual verification.

Root Cause Discussion:
TK-301's formal annual inspection (which includes level gauge
calibration verification per SOP-TK-011) has been overdue since
2025-01-15 (see IR-TK301-2024-01 and ML-TK301-2025-08-30). While a
direct causal link to this specific gauge discrepancy cannot be
confirmed without the overdue inspection, the timing raises the
possibility that a calibration drift issue would have been caught
earlier had the inspection been completed on schedule.

Corrective Actions:
1. Manual dip checks to continue for all TK-301 fills until formal
   inspection is completed.
2. Overdue inspection escalated to EHS lead as a priority scheduling
   item given this near-miss.

Reported per SOP-INC-001.
