NEAR-MISS REPORT
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Report #: INC-2025-019
Date of Event: 2025-08-22
Reported by: David Kim
Equipment Involved: FN-33 (Cooling Tower Fan 33)

Description:
Operator on routine rounds noticed light smoke and an unusual odor
near the FN-33 motor housing. Fan was stopped immediately and isolated
before any fire or further damage occurred.

Potential Consequence:
Continued operation could have led to bearing seizure, motor winding
damage, or in a worst case a localized fire. Prompt operator
identification prevented escalation.

Root Cause Discussion:
Bearing overheating from grease breakdown, consistent with an
incorrect grease substitution (same NLGI-1 substitute pattern found on
AC-12 in June 2025). This confirms a plant-wide pattern rather than an
isolated equipment issue - two separate rotating equipment items with
the same root cause within a three-month window.

Corrective Actions:
1. Bearing replaced with correct grease grade (WO-10221).
2. Escalated to reliability team for a lessons-learned review across
   all rotating equipment PMs performed during the period grease
   stockouts were occurring - see INC-2025-025.

Reported per SOP-INC-001.
