INCIDENT REPORT
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Report #: INC-2025-013
Date of Event: 2025-06-11
Reported by: David Kim
Equipment Involved: AC-12 (Air Compressor 12)

Description:
AC-12 tripped on high bearing temperature alarm during normal
operation, automatically shutting down. Backup compressor took over
plant air load with no interruption to production. No injury.

Potential Consequence:
Had the backup compressor not been available or had a delayed
response occurred, loss of plant air could have affected
instrumentation and pneumatic valve operation plant-wide.

Root Cause Discussion:
Bearing overheating traced to incorrect grease (NLGI-1 substitute
used during a prior PM due to stockout of the specified NLGI-2 grade
per SOP-AC-018). Substitution was not logged or flagged for follow-up
at the time it was made.

Corrective Actions:
1. Bearing replaced with correct grease grade (WO-10218).
2. Flagged to stores/planning: grease substitutions require supervisor
   approval and logging going forward.
3. Similar pattern later identified on FN-33 (Aug 2025) - see lessons-
   learned report INC-2025-025.

Reported per SOP-INC-001.
