NEAR-MISS REPORT
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Report #: INC-2026-004
Date of Event: 2026-03-05
Reported by: Sarah Chen
Equipment Involved: P-104 (Centrifugal Pump, Unit 3)

Description:
During walkdown inspection, mechanical seal leak was observed on P-104
(third occurrence of this failure mode - see ML-P104-2026-03-05).
Small amount of process water pooled near the pump base before
isolation. No injury occurred; minor slip hazard was identified and
addressed by wiping down the area immediately.

Potential Consequence:
If unaddressed, continued leakage could have created a larger slip
hazard in a walkway area, or led to a larger loss of containment if
the seal failed catastrophically rather than gradually.

Root Cause Discussion:
This is the third seal failure on P-104 within 12 months, all with the
same thermal root cause (seal material not rated for current operating
temperature). Prior two occurrences were treated as isolated repairs;
this event prompted the permanent fix (upgraded SiC/SiC seal, see
WO-10298) rather than another like-for-like replacement.

Corrective Actions:
1. Permanent seal upgrade completed same day (WO-10298).
2. Recommend SOP-MP-014 be updated to specify high-temp seal option
   explicitly for pumps in elevated-temperature service, to prevent
   recurrence on similar equipment.

Reported per SOP-INC-001.
