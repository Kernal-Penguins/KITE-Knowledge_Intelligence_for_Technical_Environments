# SOP-TK-011: Storage Tank Inspection Procedure

**Revision:** 2  |  **Effective Date:** 2020-04-01  |  **Owner:** EHS / Asset Integrity

## 1. Purpose
Defines the annual inspection requirements for atmospheric storage
tanks at Cedar Point Processing Facility.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all raw material and product storage tanks, including
TK-301.

## 3. Inspection Frequency
Full external inspection required annually. Internal inspection
required per API 653 risk-based schedule (not covered by this SOP).

## 4. Procedure
1. Visually inspect tank shell for corrosion, coating failure, or
   deformation.
2. Inspect foundation for settling or cracking.
3. Check all fittings, valves, and connections for leaks.
4. Verify level gauge calibration against a manual reference
   measurement.
5. Document findings on the standard tank inspection form with a
   pass/fail result and next-due date.
6. Any overdue inspection must be escalated to the EHS lead and
   flagged in the maintenance log system until scheduled.

## 5. Notes
Overdue tank inspections are a compliance finding and must not be
allowed to lapse more than 60 days past the due date without EHS
lead approval and documented justification.
