# SOP-AC-018: Rotating Equipment Lubrication Standard

**Revision:** 2  |  **Effective Date:** 2022-02-01  |  **Owner:** Maintenance Engineering

## 1. Purpose
Establishes the standard grease specification for rotating equipment
bearings plant-wide, to ensure consistent lubrication practice across
pumps, fans, compressors, and motors.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all rotating equipment bearings unless a specific
manufacturer specification states otherwise.

## 3. Standard Grease Specification
Grease grade **NLGI-2**, lithium-complex base, is the standard
specification for general rotating equipment bearings at this
facility.

**Substitution policy:** If the specified NLGI-2 grease is not
available in stores, technicians must obtain supervisor approval
before substituting an alternate grade or base oil, and the
substitution must be logged on the work order. Substitutions should
be treated as temporary and followed up with correct-grade
re-lubrication at the next opportunity.

## 4. Procedure
1. Confirm correct grease grade against equipment nameplate/spec
   before application.
2. Apply per manufacturer-specified volume; do not over-grease.
3. Log grease grade used on the work order or maintenance log.
4. If a substitution was made, flag for follow-up re-lubrication.

## 5. Notes
Incorrect grease grade or base oil is a known cause of premature
bearing failure through overheating and grease breakdown. Multiple
plant incidents (see incident reports) have traced bearing failures
back to unlogged or unapproved grease substitutions during stockouts.
