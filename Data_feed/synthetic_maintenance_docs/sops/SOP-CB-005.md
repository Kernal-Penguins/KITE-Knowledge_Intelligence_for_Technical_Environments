# SOP-CB-005: Conveyor Belt Drive Lubrication and Bearing Maintenance

**Revision:** 4  |  **Effective Date:** 2021-09-01  |  **Owner:** Maintenance Engineering

## 1. Purpose
Establishes the standard lubrication and bearing inspection schedule
for conveyor belt drive motors.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all belt conveyor drive assemblies plant-wide.

## 3. Standard Lubrication Interval
Drive motor bearings shall be greased every 3 months (standard duty,
defined as less than 12 hours/day operation) using NLGI-2 grade grease
per SOP-AC-018.

**Note (added Rev. 4):** Conveyors operating at high utilization
(greater than 15 hours/day) should follow an accelerated interval of
6 weeks rather than the standard 3-month interval. Individual
equipment PM schedules should be adjusted accordingly by the planner;
this base SOP interval remains the default for standard-duty
conveyors pending a full interval study.

## 4. Procedure
1. Isolate conveyor per SOP-LOTO-002 before servicing.
2. Wipe grease fitting clean before applying new grease.
3. Apply grease per manufacturer volume specification; do not
   over-grease.
4. Check for bearing play by hand before and after service.
5. Record bearing temperature and vibration reading if test equipment
   is available.
6. Log service date to maintain accurate interval tracking.

## 5. Notes
Grinding noise, elevated bearing temperature (>160F), or vibration
above 0.12 ips should be treated as an early warning sign requiring
immediate inspection, not deferral to the next scheduled PM.
