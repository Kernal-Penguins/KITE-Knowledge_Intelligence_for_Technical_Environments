MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: P104 (Centrifugal Pump, Unit 3)
Date: 2026-03-05
Logged by: James Whitfield

Description:
Third mechanical seal failure on this pump within 12 months. Leak detected
during Sarah Chen's walkdown inspection this morning; pump isolated
immediately given prior history and proximity to walkway.

Findings:
Same glazing/scoring pattern on carbon face as prior two failures. This
confirms the root cause is thermal - standard carbon/ceramic seal is not
adequate for current discharge temperature on Unit 3. Reliability
engineering review (requested Sept 2025) was never completed.

Action Taken:
Replaced with upgraded silicon carbide/silicon carbide seal faces rated
to 300F (vs. prior 220F rating) - parts expedited from vendor. Pump
returned to service 2026-03-05 1600. This is intended as the permanent
fix per WO-10298. Will monitor over next 2 quarterly inspections to
confirm resolution.

Status: Resolved - Root cause addressed (see WO-10298)
