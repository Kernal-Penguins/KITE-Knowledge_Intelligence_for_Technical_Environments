MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: CV-220
Date: 2026-06-09
Logged by: James Whitfield

Description:
Third positioner failure on CV-220 following heavy rain, consistent
with prior two occurrences (May 2025, Nov 2025). Valve stuck at ~35%
open, cooling water flow reduced to Unit 2.

Findings:
Confirmed root cause: NEMA 3R positioner enclosure is not adequate for
this valve's outdoor, rain-exposed location. Two prior temporary
repairs (gasket replacement) did not address underlying issue.

Action Taken:
Isolated and dried positioner, temporary gasket repair performed to
restore service. Requested NEMA 4X replacement enclosure be expedited
per recommendation logged in Nov 2025 - see WO-10247 and WO-10312.
This is the permanent fix scheduled for next outage window.

Status: Resolved (temporary) - Permanent fix pending parts, 3rd occurrence
