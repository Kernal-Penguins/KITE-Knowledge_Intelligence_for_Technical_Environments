MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: FN-33 (Cooling Tower Fan 33)
Date: 2025-08-22
Logged by: David Kim

Description:
Operator reported light smoke/odor near FN-33 motor housing. Fan
stopped immediately, isolated for inspection. See INC-2025-019 for
near-miss reporting.

Findings:
Bearing overheating, similar grease degradation pattern to AC-12
(June 2025). Confirmed same substitute grease (NLGI-1, wrong base oil)
was used on FN-33's last PM - same stores stockout issue.

Action Taken:
Bearing replaced, correct NLGI-2 grease applied. WO-10221 opened.
Combined with AC-12 finding, this confirms a plant-wide issue: grease
substitutions during stockouts are not being tracked or flagged across
multiple rotating equipment PMs. Recommend reliability review - see
lessons-learned report INC-2025-025.

Status: Resolved
