MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: Belt Drive CB-15
Date: 2026-05-14
Logged by: Robert Alvarez

Description:
Third drive motor bearing failure on CB-15. Caught during scheduled
inspection (see IR-CB15-2026-02) which had flagged early bearing wear
signs; failure occurred before the recommended follow-up interval.

Findings:
Root cause confirmed as inadequate lubrication interval for this duty
cycle - the 3-month schedule in SOP-CB-005 was written for standard
duty but CB-15 runs at high utilization (>20 hrs/day). Two prior
repairs (Apr 2025, Oct 2025) treated the symptom, not the schedule
mismatch.

Action Taken:
Bearing replaced. WO-10305 opened. PM schedule for CB-15 permanently
revised to 6-week lubrication interval, tracked separately from the
standard SOP-CB-005 schedule until the SOP itself is updated for
high-duty conveyors.

Status: Resolved - Root cause addressed via schedule change (3rd occurrence)
