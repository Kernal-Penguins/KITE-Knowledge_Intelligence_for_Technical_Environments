MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: CB-15 (Conveyor Belt Drive 15, Raw Material Feed)
Date: 2025-04-25
Logged by: Maria Torres, Maintenance Supervisor

Description:
Operators reported unusual grinding noise from CB-15 drive motor during
morning startup. Conveyor shut down for inspection before noise
worsened.

Findings:
Drive motor bearing showing significant wear, some play detectable by
hand. Bearing temp elevated (measured 185F vs normal ~140F) prior to
shutdown. Lubrication records show last grease application was 5 months
prior - interval exceeds the 3-month schedule in SOP-CB-005 for this
duty cycle.

Action Taken:
Bearing replaced, drive motor regreased. WO-10202 opened. Flagged PM
schedule gap to planning - lubrication interval appears to not be
enforced consistently on this line.

Status: Resolved (temporary)
