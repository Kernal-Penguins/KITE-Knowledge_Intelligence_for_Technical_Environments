MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: CV-220 (Control Valve, Unit 2 Cooling Water Loop)
Date: 2025-05-20
Logged by: Robert Alvarez, Maintenance Technician

Description:
Valve failed to respond to control signal during heavy rain event
overnight. Operations noted CV-220 stuck at ~40% open regardless of
setpoint changes. Cooling water flow to Unit 2 was affected for approx
45 minutes before technician arrived.

Findings:
Pneumatic positioner cover gasket appears degraded, allowing water
ingress into positioner housing during heavy rain. Internal components
showed corrosion/moisture damage.

Action Taken:
Positioner dried out, gasket replaced, valve function tested and
confirmed normal. WO-10205 opened. Recommend checking positioner
enclosure rating - may not be adequate for outdoor exposure at this
location.

Status: Resolved (temporary)
