MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: AC-12 (Air Compressor 12, Plant Air System)
Date: 2025-06-11
Logged by: David Kim

Description:
AC-12 tripped on high bearing temperature alarm at 0940. Compressor
automatically shut down; plant air backup compressor took over load.

Findings:
Drive-end bearing found overheated, grease sample showed signs of
breakdown/discoloration inconsistent with the NLGI-2 grease specified.
Records show maintenance crew used a general-purpose grease (NLGI-1,
different base oil) from stores due to the specified grease being out
of stock at time of last PM.

Action Taken:
Bearing replaced, correct grease grade applied per SOP-AC-018. WO-10218
opened. Flagged to stores/planning that grease substitutions on
rotating equipment PMs need supervisor approval - see also FN-33 log
from August with same root cause.

Status: Resolved
