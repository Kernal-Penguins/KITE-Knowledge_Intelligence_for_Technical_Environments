MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: Control Valve 220
Date: 2025-11-14
Logged by: R. Alvarez

Description:
CV220 again failed to track setpoint following overnight rain, same
symptom as May incident. Valve found stuck at partial open position.

Findings:
Moisture ingress into positioner again, gasket in poor condition. This
is the second such failure this year, both following heavy rain events.
Enclosure rating question raised in May was never resolved - positioner
model installed is rated NEMA 3R, likely insufficient for direct
outdoor exposure at this location.

Action Taken:
Dried and reset positioner, replaced gasket again. WO-10247 opened,
flagged for reliability engineering review of positioner enclosure
spec. This is a recurring issue - recommend upgrade to NEMA 4X housing.

Status: Resolved (temporary) - Recurring issue, 2nd occurrence
