MAINTENANCE LOG ENTRY
Facility: Cedar Point Processing Facility
Equipment: P-104 (Centrifugal Pump, Unit 3 - Process Water Transfer)
Date: 2025-04-12
Logged by: James Whitfield, Senior Maintenance Technician
Shift: Day

Description:
Operator reported visible leakage from mechanical seal area on P-104 during
routine rounds (0630). Inspected pump on arrival; steady drip from seal
housing, approx 1 drop/sec. No abnormal vibration or noise. Pump shut down
and isolated per LOTO-002 at 0715 for seal inspection.

Findings:
Mechanical seal faces show scoring consistent with dry-running/heat damage.
Carbon seal face appears glazed. Process fluid temp at this pump has been
running ~15F above the original design basis since the Unit 3 upgrade last
year - suspect seal material may not be rated for current service temp.

Action Taken:
Seal replaced in kind (standard carbon/ceramic set) from stores. Pump
returned to service at 1130. Recommend engineering review seal material
selection given elevated operating temp. Work order WO-10201 opened for
tracking.

Status: Resolved (temporary) - Follow-up recommended
